Market Analysis

Advantages of Energy Powders

  • Convenience: Easy to transport, store, and use (just add water)
  • Effectiveness: Provides quick energy release and supports productivity
  • Flexibility: Allows users to adjust the dosage according to their needs
  • Cost-efficiency: Compared to ready-to-drink energy beverages, powders are generally more affordable

Disadvantages of Energy Powders

  • Taste: Not all formulas have a pleasant flavor, which can impact the product's attractiveness
  • Risk of Overdose: Without proper labeling, consumers may exceed the recommended dosage
